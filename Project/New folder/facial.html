Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG : University Blue

View Larger Picture

The Air Jordan 1 has become Jordan Brand‘s pièce de résistance over the last few years, and that momentum
appears as if it’ll roll right along in 2021 thanks to models like the “University Blue.” Blending classic colorblocking,
an eponymous shade of blue that seemingly pulls from Michael Jordan‘s collegiate days with the UNC Tar Heels and an
alternate material construction, the “University Blue” supplies an appealing blend of fresh and familiar.

The all-leather upper features a white base with powder blue overlays and a black signature Swoosh. Matching black accents
make their way to the collar, tongue tag and printed Wings logo on the lateral collar flap. A brighter shade of blue is applied
to the standard AJ1 outsole, featuring multi-directional traction and a pivot point under the forefoot.
