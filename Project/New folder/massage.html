Climate change refers to long-term variations in Earth's temperature, precipitation, and other atmospheric conditions.
With rising temperatures, rising sea levels, and an in-crease in the frequency of extreme weather events, it is imperative
that we confront and lessen the effects of climate change. Since addressing climate change impacts not only the environment
but also the moral and existential well-being of present and future generations, it is a significant problem that demands quick and
coordinated action. Scientists are very confident that the greenhouse gases produced by human activity will be the
primary cause of the prolonged rise in global temperature over the coming decades.
